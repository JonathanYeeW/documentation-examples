# Strategic

Documents that define why the company exists, where it's going, and what it values. These are the highest-altitude documents in the company — they set direction that everything else flows from. A new hire should be able to read the strategic docs and understand the soul of the company without touching a single technical document.

## Audience

Everyone. Leadership writes these, but the whole company reads them. They should be accessible to anyone regardless of role or technical background.

## Document Types

| Document | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Product Vision | Why the company exists, what future we're building toward, and the strategic bets to get there | ✅ `pv_pbj-co.md` |
| Company Values / Operating Principles | How we work together — the behavioral expectations and cultural commitments that shape daily decisions | — |
| OKRs / Goals | What we're trying to achieve this quarter or year, measured concretely | — |
