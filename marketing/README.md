# Marketing

Documents that explain the product to people who don't use it yet — or who use it but haven't put words to why it matters. These aren't ads. They're honest, specific stories and explanations that help someone understand what the product does, why it exists, and what it feels like to use it. The best marketing docs don't feel like marketing. They feel like a smart friend explaining something useful.

## Audience

Prospective users, curious observers, and people who have heard about the product secondhand and want to understand it. These documents are written for people on the outside looking in — they should require no prior knowledge of the product.

## Document Types

| Document | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Case Study | A real or illustrative story of someone using the product to solve a specific problem — what they were dealing with, how the product helped, and what the outcome was | ✅ `cs_sandwich-emergency.md` |
| Explainer | A plain-language overview of what the product does and why someone would want it — the answer to "so what is this, exactly?" | — |
| Feature Highlight | A focused document on one specific feature — what it does, who it's for, and what it unlocks | — |
