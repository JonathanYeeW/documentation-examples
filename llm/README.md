# LLM

Documents designed to be produced by an LLM as part of a defined workflow. These aren't documents a person could easily write by hand — they depend on the LLM synthesizing a persistent knowledge base, surfacing gaps, and structuring output for immediate use. The LLM isn't just helping write them; it's the mechanism that makes them possible at all.

## Audience

The individual running the workflow. These documents are personal and operational — produced right before an interaction, consumed during it, then superseded by the next one. They're not archived for teams or referenced months later. Their shelf life is a single session.

## Document Types

| Document | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Prep Doc | An LLM-synthesized briefing produced immediately before a recurring interaction. Derived from a persistent knowledge base. Orients the person in context and structures delivery as minimal anchor points to riff from. Ephemeral by design. | `prep_account-checkin.md` |
